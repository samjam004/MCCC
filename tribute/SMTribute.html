T1 Faker

Faker, a.k.a. Lee Sang-hyeok, is one who many would consider to be the greatest League of Legends player of all time.

Background

Lee Sang-hyeok, aka Faker, was born May 7th, 1996 in South Korea. He attended Mapo High School, but soon dropped out to join SKT T1 K and start as their midlaner. His debut let him showcase his absolute dominance on Nidalee, landing spears with precision against some of the greatest players of the time. In 2015 he won his first championship. He went on to win 2 more along with multiple LCK finals, including one in 2021, and is still playing as part owner of T1 eSports.
